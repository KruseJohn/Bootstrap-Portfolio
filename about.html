About Me

Full-stack web developer with extensive background as a magnetic resonance imaging technologist. Not long ago, I retired from the healthcare industry after 25 rewarding years. Wanting to remain in a technical field, and knowing I have a lot to contribute, I began exploring computer programming as this has always piqued my interest. After extensive research, I decided to enroll in a full-stack web development program offered at Denver University. Through the program, I have become skilled with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, NodeJS, and Bootstrap. This, along with my reputation as a self-directed, detail oriented professional with superior problem solving, communication, and management skills, has prepared me for a successful transition into a Web Development position. This career is how I intend to spend the remainder of my working life and I look forward to an exciting future with many options!
