About Me

Hello!

My name is John. Not long ago, I retired from the healthcare industry after 25 rewarding years as a Magnetic Resonance Imaging Technologist. I fiddled around with different smaller jobs mostly working outdoors, but soon felt I was missing something. Knowing I still have a lot to contribute, the ambitious side of me started snooping around computer programming as this has always piqued my interest. After a little research, I decided to enroll in a Full-Stack Web Development program offered at Denver University. I hope to find a career in web development after successful completion of the course. Ideally, I would love to find an employer who will allow me to work remotely. This career is how I intend to spend the remainder of my working life and I look forward to an exciting future with many options!
